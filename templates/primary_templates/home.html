Welcome to Dancexam

Dancexam is your go-to digital platform for grading dance professionals. Originally built for the Greenville Westies, our website provides an efficient way for instructors to input and manage scores during advancement tests.

How It Works

Dancexam simplifies the grading process. Instructors can easily log in, select the testee, and digitally input scores for each dance level. The platform automatically calculates final grades and provides instant feedback, making it easier to track progress and ensure consistent evaluation.
